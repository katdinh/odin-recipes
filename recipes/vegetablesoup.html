Vegetable Soup

Soup for the soul

This is a blended vegetable soup perfect for rainy fall days.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 medium-sized carrots
  • 2-3 celery stalks
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or avocado oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut carrots and celery stalks into 1-2 inches.
  2. Remove ginger skin.
  3. Sautee carrots and celery until slightly browned and tender.
  4. Add water just enough to cover vegetables in pot.
  5. Add ginger and garlic to pot.
  6. Let simmer for 30 minutes until all ingredients are combined well.
  7. Put soup in a blender and blend until all ingredients become a creamy liquid.
  8. Salt and pepper to taste.
